Michael's Provision Recipe

Back



Description

This provision recipe makes for a good breakfast. Packed with nutrients, protein, and a variety of different foods that hold their own benefits, this dish will keep you full for a majority of the day.

Ingredients

  • Kingfish
  • All purpose seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Bananas
  • Yams
  • Potatoes
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Clean the fish thoroughly. Wash and scale your fish.
  2. Season with all purpose seaosning, black pepper, salt, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  3. Oil a pan and start a fire on medium.
  4. Place fish in the pan after it's heated.
  5. Set a pot to boil for the remaining ingredients and fill it halfway with water.
  6. Slit the bananas 3 ways so when they boil you can peel the skin off easily.
  7. Peel and cut the yams and add to the boiling pot.
  8. Peel and cut the potatoes and add to the pot.
  9. Add bananas to the pot.
  10. Mix flour water and salt in a bowl for dumplings
  11. Add dumplings to boiling pot.
  12. Leave the pot to boil for 45 minutes and then your meal is done.
