Michael's Stew Pea Recipe

Back



Description

This stew pea recipe is a deceptively easy meal to put together, so don't be offput by the list of ingedients. This is a meal that can feed a family of five over two days, depending on how much you guys can eat in one serving, and all out of one pot!


Ingredients

  • 3 scallions
  • 1/2 an onion
  • Coconut cream
  • 2 tbsp all purpose seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Kidney beans
  • Turkey necks
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with 3 cups of kidney beans and fill the pot halfway with water and set the fire to high heat.
  2. Clean your scallions with water and cut the roots and dead leaves off. Cut the scallions in half and add them in the water.
  3. Peel and chop half an onion and add it to the pot.
  4. Add a bit of coconut cream to the pot.
  5. Sprinkle two tablespoons of all purpose seasoning to the pot.
  6. Add garlic powder and salt to taste. Check every 5 minutes until seasoned to your liking.
  7. Check to see when the peas are soft by pressing them with a fork until you find that they are easy to pierce.
  8. When the water starts to boil, turn the temperature to medium.
  9. Once peas are soft, put your turkey necks in the pot and cover the top. Make sure the water is still boiling lower than the top of the pot.
  10. To make the dumplings for this meal ix flour water and salt in a bowl for dumplings. Pull pieces of dough off and roll into small cylindrical shapes and place in pot to cook.
  11. Once the water in the pot has turned red and started to thicken from the seasoning, meats, and vegetables, turn the water low to a simmer until stew thickens to your desired amount.
