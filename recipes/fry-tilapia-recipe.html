Michael's Fried Tilapia



Description

The ingredients you need to make your own fried fish meal are not hard to acquire. However, this recipe does not use exact measurements.
You must rely on your own expertise to determine how you would like to season your fish. In practicing this form of creative expression you get the opportunity to tailor your meal to your specific wants and needs. This meal serves five.


Ingredients

  • Lime juice
  • Tilapia
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Paprika
  • Large ziploc (or plastic) bag

Recipe

  1. Soak your tilapia in a bowl of water and lime to clean your fish.
  2. Cut your fish into sections for frying.
  3. Fill a frying pan up with 1 inch of oil covering the entire surface and heat it over a high fire to heat up the oil.
  4. In your ziploc bag, combine the garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, flour, cornmeal, and paprika together. Close and shake your bag to mix the seasoning.
  5. Place a cut of your fish in the bag and shake well to cover the entire fish. Place your seasoned fish in the frying pan. Repeat until your pan is filled with fish.
  6. Watch your fish in the pan and turn every 1-2 minutes until your fish is lightly browned and crispy.
